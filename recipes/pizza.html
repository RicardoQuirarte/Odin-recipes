Gourmet White Pizza

Yummy! This is a wonderful pizza for those who are looking to get

away from the basic, cheese and pepperoni pizza.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 3 tablespoons minced garlic
  • 2 tablespoons sun-dried tomato pesto
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 tablespoon grated Parmesan cheese

Steps to be make

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).

  2. a small bowl, mix together the butter, olive oil, garlic, pesto, basil,

    oregano, Parmesan cheese and Alfredo sauce. Arrange the chicken on top of

    the pizza crust. Pour the Alfredo sauce mixture evenly over the chicken.

    Top with tomato and feta cheese.

  3. Bake for 10 to 15 minutes in the preheated oven, until the crust is lightly

    browned andtoppings are toasted. Cut into wedges to serve.
